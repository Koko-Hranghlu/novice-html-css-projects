Pollution And Pollution Control

Pollution is the introduction of contaminants into the natural environment that cause adverse change. Pollution can take the form of chemical substances or energy, such as noise, heat or light. Pollutants the components of pollution can be either foreign substances/energies or naturally occurring contaminants. Pollution is classified as point-source or non-point-source pollution.

There are mainly four types of pollution shown:

  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Soil Pollution
  • Noise Pollution
